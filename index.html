What is graphic designing ?

Graphic design is the art and practice of planning and projecting ideas and experiences with visual and textual content. It's a form of visual communication that uses images, typography, and layout to convey messages and information. Essentially, graphic designers create visuals to communicate specific ideas or concepts.
