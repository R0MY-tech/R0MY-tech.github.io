  • Vector-based system: Allows for infinite scalability and crispness without losing quality, ideal for logos and graphics used in various sizes.
  • Comprehensive toolset: Offers diverse tools for drawing, shaping, and coloring, enabling intricate and customized designs.
  • Professional-level precision: Provides exacting detail and control, favored by professional designers for polished graphics.
  • Integration with Adobe products:Seamlessly works with applications like Photoshop and InDesign, enhancing design workflow.
  • Industry standard: Widely used in the design industry, making it an essential skill for designers and often a job requirement.
