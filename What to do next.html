What to do after ?

After learning Adobe graphic design tools like Photoshop, Illustrator, or InDesign, you can begin building your career through various opportunities. As a junior graphic designer working part-time, you can typically earn between $15 to $25 per hour, depending on your location and skill level. Full-time junior designers often earn a starting salary of $35,000 to $50,000 per year in many countries, with higher pay in major cities or tech-driven industries. You can choose to freelance on platforms like Upwork or Fiverr, apply for agency or in-house roles, or keep improving your skills through online courses and certifications. Over time, this path can lead to higher-paying positions like UI/UX designer, motion designer, or creative director, and even open doors to launching your own freelance business or design studio. The field of graphic design offers not just creative freedom, but also solid income growth and long-term potential.
